Work History

Owen A Podziemski started his work journey as a Sales Consultant at Office Max where he was able to crack and break out of his shell in customer service and professional communication. This development continued as he got his first internship at a local manufacturing company, Galland Henning Nopak. There he supported the finance and sales departments through paperwork processing but was also given freedom to explore implementation of new systems for organization and efficiency which helped streamline paperwork-heavy processes. Transitioning into college, as Owen was studying for his first two exams he spent a summer working at Landmark Credit Union. He sought out this position because if he was not yet ready for actuarial work, he wanted to still be somewhere still relevant to the financial world. He worked very closely with management here to go above and beyond his position. He showed his job the same dedication he would show his studies, allowing him to advance quickly and take on extra work to relieve some of the burden from tasks that management had assumed due to staffing shortages.
